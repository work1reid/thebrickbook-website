PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

TheBrickBook ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. DATA WE COLLECT

When you use TheBrickBook, we may collect:

  • Account information: Email address, name, phone number, physical address, and other contact details you provide when setting up your business profile.
  • Business data: ABN, company name, bank details, and other information you enter for invoices.
  • Client information: Names, contact details, and addresses of clients you add to the app, including contacts imported from your device.
  • Job and invoice data: Details of jobs, quotes, expenses, timers, and invoices you create.
  • User ID: A unique identifier for your account.
  • Device ID and advertising data: Used by our advertising partners to display relevant ads.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your data to:

  • Provide app functionality (job tracking, invoicing, expense management)
  • Sync your data across devices when you sign in
  • Display advertisements via third-party ad networks
  • Improve the app and fix bugs

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and data storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. Google Privacy Policy

4. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., Supabase)
  • Google AdMob for displaying ads
  • Law enforcement if required by law

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to the cloud when you create an account.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your data within the app
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Delete your account at any time

7. GOVERNING LAW

This privacy policy is governed by the laws of New South Wales, Australia, and the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).
